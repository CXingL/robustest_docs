Getting Started
===============
 .. image:: _static/adminserver.png


The first step to using RobusTest platform is to create an account for yourself.
If you are using the public instance at http://robustest.com, you can easily create an account for yourself from the Sign Up page.

Please note that you can sign up on the public platform with a business email address.
On Sign Up, your account is credited with 60 mins of device usage with an overall cap of 180 minutes on the organization.

Once your account is created, you can sign in to the platform to start using RobusTest.
